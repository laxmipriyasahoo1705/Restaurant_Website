About Delight Restaurant

Delight Restaurant is a premium restaurant that serves authentic and delicious delicacies. It is present at Jhansinagar,Gandhi Street, Gujarat. It has also a Terrace Cafe that serves Indian cuisine. Wonderful service, very friendly staff, and a huge selection of refreshing beverages. You can find a wide variety of vegetarian and non-vegetarian options along with variety of beverages. It provide various cuisines with an aesthetic seating arrangement. A good place to hang out with friends or even dine in with family. We also provide services such as Counter Service, Catering Services and Home Delivery facilities.
